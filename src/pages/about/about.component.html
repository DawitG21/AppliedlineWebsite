Appliedline™ is the software development branch of AA Advanced Information Technology PLC® (AAAIT), specialized in software development for goverment, corporate and individual organizations.

AAAIT PLC has been in the business of IT hardware and service deliverables since 2006 before incubating its software development unit in 2009. In 2015, AAAIT PLC decided to let its software development unit run independently as Appliedline with a vision to be incorporated by 2020.

Our core values are precision, synergy, professionalism and continuous learning. Our goal is to exceed the expectations of every client by offering outstanding customer service and great value that puts their bussiness at a competitive advantage as well as eliminate outlined bussiness problems.

At Appliedline, we value our customers hence we invest a lot in hiring developers that have solid and vast experience in the software development industry. Our team have deep industry knowledge in technologies such as ASP.NET MVC, .NET Core, AngularJS & Angular, NodeJS, cross-platform and hybrid app development (Xamarin and Cordova), UX and UI design, RDBMS & NoSql databases, Microsoft Azure and many more.

In order to meet the demand of our ever growing clientele, Appliedline maintains a wide range of technology development programmes to keep its employees up-to-date with the latest tools and resources in the software development industry.

Technical expertise along with vast and deep industry knowledge makes Appliedline the ideal choice for a software development company to provide custom software services and products to your company.
